Innovation

The widespread adoption of mobile banking and digital wallets like bKash, Nagad, and Rocket has revolutionized financial inclusion, allowing millions of unbanked citizens to access financial services.

Sustainability is a critical focus for Bangladesh as it strives to balance economic growth with environmental preservation and social equity.
